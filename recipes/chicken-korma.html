Chicken Korma

Description

This sped-up version of chicken korma saves time without sacrificing flavor! Boneless chicken thighs are gently simmered in a rich, creamy sauce to create layers of sweet and spicy flavors.

Ingredients

  • 12 to 15 raw almonds
  • 2 to 3 green cardamom pods
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly cracked peppercorn
  • 1-inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 to 3 whole cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon shredded coconut, optional
  • Silvered almonds and chopped cilantro, for garnish
